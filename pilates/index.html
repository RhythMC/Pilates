Pilates re:FORMER Class 1

Opening Movement

Leg & Footwork

3 Red Heavy

  • Strengthen the leg muscles including the internal and external rotators, quadriceps, hamstrings, abductor, adductors, calf muscles and ankle stabilizers
  • Develop support for neutral spine
  • Teach lumbopelvic stability
  • Correct hip, leg and ankle alignment

Pelvic Press

3 Red Heavy

  • Strengthen gluteus maximus, hamstrings, and back extensors
  • Increase flexibility and articulation of the spine
  • Teach balanced abdominal and lumbar strength

Supine Abdominals

1 Red 1 Blue Light

  • Strengthen abdominals
  • Strengthen the arms
  • Maintain neutral spine
  • Connect the movement of the arms to the core

Feet in the Straps

1 Red 1 Blue Light

  • Strengthen abdominals
  • Strengthen hamstrings, adductors, deep hip external rotators, gluteus maximus and hip abductors
  • Increase flexibility of the hamstrings, adductors, and hip flexors
  • Promotes pelvic stability

Seated Mermaid

1 Red 1 Blue Light

  • Stretch the lateral torso including quadratus lumborum, lattisimus dorsi, internal and external obliques, and intercostals
  • Stabilize the scapula

Roll Backs 1

1 Red 1 Blue Light

  • Strengthen abdominals
  • Stretch the lower back
  • Maintain abdominal support for the low back while in lumbar flexion

Arm Work Forward

1 Blue Light

  • Strengthen arm and shoulder muscles including brachialis, biceps, triceps, pectorals, latissimus dorsi, rotator cuff, and deltoids.
  • Strengthen scapular stabilizers
  • Increase torso stabilization
  • Improve sitting posture

Arm Work Prone

1 Blue Light

  • Strengthen the back of the body including the latissimus dorsi, teres major, lower trapezius, erector spinae, gluteus maximus, and hamstrings
  • Strengthen abdominals in prone position
  • Increase scapular stability
  • Improve leg and torso alignment

Eve's Lunge

1 Blue

  • Strengthen the gluet medius
  • Maintain spinal flexion and stable pelvis while stretching the psoas
  • Achieve full flexion of the abdonimals
